B91 is a carefully curated apartment on Via Francesco Brioschi, steps from Milan's iconic Navigli canals. We are Vincenzo and Ilenia — and B91 is the place we've poured our attention into so that every guest feels genuinely welcomed.

No agency. No anonymity. When you book with us, you book direct — and you'll always have a real person to reach.
